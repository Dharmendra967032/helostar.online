## ğŸ“± Step-by-Step Guide

### Watching a Short
1. **Open Helostar**
2. **Video plays automatically** when you scroll to it
3. **Enjoy!** No need to press play

### Muting Audio
1. **Tap LEFT 1/3 of screen**
2. **Volume icon appears**
3. **Audio mutes/unmutes**

### Liking a Video
1. **Double-tap ANYWHERE on video**
2. **Heart emoji appears at tap location**
3. **Heart animates and fades away**
4. **Like count increases**
5. **Dark red â¤ï¸ appears on like button**

### Going Fullscreen
1. **Tap CENTER or RIGHT of screen**
2. **All UI disappears** (header, buttons, etc)
3. **Video fills entire screen**
4. **Description shows at bottom** (semi-transparent)
5. **Tap again to exit fullscreen**

### Auto-Scroll Feature
1. **Video finishes playing**
2. **Automatically scrolls to next short**
3. **Next video auto-plays**
4. **No manual scrolling needed!**

### Following a Creator
1. **Tap "Follow" button** (in header)
2. **Button changes to "Following"**
3. **Follower count updates**
4. **You're now following!**

### Updating Your Profile Picture
1. **Tap YOUR avatar** (on your own videos only)
2. **Select new image**
3. **Image uploads and appears immediately**
